Our Story & Journey

PodNova was born out of a simple realization: podcasting workflows were broken. Creators had to juggle hosting, transcription bots, advertising agents, and email subscription tools on different platforms.

We built PodNova to unify the entire process. Today, we empower creators to record, transcribe with high-accuracy AI, distribute to all directories instantly, and insert audio promotions dynamically.

🌐 Audio delivery CDN

High Uptime Global Infrastructure

Our distributed caching network ensures your listeners experience zero buffering. With storage nodes across the US, Europe, and APAC, we guarantee high-speed downloads, no matter where your audience tunes in from.
